Our advantage

Why Superstate's tokenized funds are trusted by investors, treasuries, protocols, and intermediaries.

As fast as a blockchain

We’ve engineered our funds to allow for on-chain settlement and continuous interest accrual, to match the speed of DeFi.

  • Continuous NAV per share (NAV/S) allows for second-by-second pricing on our flagship fund, USTB

  • Yield accumulates through an incrementing NAV/S, without airdrops, distributions, or token rebasing

  • Ownership transfers as easily as an ERC-20 token transfer

  • Daily liquidity on Market Days

Built for the crypto ecosystem

Efficient collateral and yield generation for protocols, stablecoin issuers, DAOs, and automated investors.

  • Mint and burn effortlessly through the Protocol Mint and Redeem smart contracts, investor portal, or APIs

  • Purchase and redeem using USDC on Ethereum and Solana, or bank wire

  • Our funds can be held at leading custodians, in self-custody, or in book-entry at Superstate

  • Deep integrations into established venues, both on-chain and off-chain

Superior regulatory structure

We’re focused on compliance, so you can focus on utility and yield.

  • Headquartered in the U.S. with a Bankruptcy-Remote Trust Structure

  • Assets are managed and segregated by established service providers

  • Available to Qualified Purchasers in supported jurisdictions

  • Minimize counterparty risk with a customizable Allowlist
